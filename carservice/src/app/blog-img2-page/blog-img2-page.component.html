Why Your Car Needs Periodic Maintenance

1. Safety First

Regular maintenance checks, like brakes and tires, keep you safe on the road. This helps prevent accidents.

2. Better Performance

Changing the oil and air filters helps the engine run better and saves gas.

3. Longer Car Life

Fixing small problems early means your car will last longer and need fewer big repairs

4. Saving Money

Regular check-ups cost less than big repairs. Catching problems early can save a lot of money.

5. Higher Resale Value

A well-maintained car is worth more if you decide to sell it.

Key Parts of Maintenance

  • Oil and Filter Changes: Keep the engine clean and running smoothly.
  • Tire Checks: Make sure tires are safe and last longer.
  • Brake Inspections: Ensure brakes work properly.
  • Fluid Checks: Keep all car fluids at the right levels.
  • Battery Checks: Make sure the car starts easily.
  • Air Filter Replacement: Helps the engine breathe better.
  • Belt and Hose Inspections: Prevent breakdowns

When to Do Maintenance

  • Every 10,000 km: Change oil and filter.
  • Every 10000kms : Rotate tires, check brakes, and replace air filter.
  • Every 100000kms : Replace spark plugs and check the timing belt.
