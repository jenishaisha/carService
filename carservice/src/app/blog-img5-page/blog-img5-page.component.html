Why Changing Your Car Oil change Is Important

1. Get Regular Oil Changes:

Make sure to change your car’s oil regularly. How often? Most cars need an oil change every 10000km . Check your car’s manual to know for sure. This keeps the engine clean and running smoothly.

2. Learn How to Change Oil:

You can learn how to change the oil yourself! First, you need the right tools and a new oil filter. You’ll drain the old oil, replace the filter, and then pour in the new oil. It’s a bit messy but not too hard.
